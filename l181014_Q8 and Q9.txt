When the inheritance becomes protected,the public and protected members becomes protected while 
the private members are hidden .

In this case when we changes the inheritance from public to protected the member variable of age
remains protected while first and last name becomes hidden.

All the public functions of person class becomes protected in the class of student and faculty so
they can not be used in main.

Q9)
When the inheritance becomes private,the public and protected members becomes private while 
the private members are hidden .

In this case when we changes the inheritance from public to private the member variable of age
becomes private while first and last name becomes hidden.

All the public functions of person class becomes private in the class of student and faculty so
they can not be used in main.
